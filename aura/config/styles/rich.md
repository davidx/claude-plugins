# Rich Style

Luxurious, detail-oriented design with depth and polish

## Characteristics
- Layered depth with shadows and gradients
- Rich color palette with multiple accents
- Decorative elements welcome
- Generous imagery and illustrations
- Animated flourishes
- Premium feel

## Colors
- Gradients encouraged (subtle)
- Deep, saturated primary colors
- Gold/warm accents for luxury feel
- Dark modes with deep blues/purples

## Typography
- Mix of serif (headings) and sans-serif (body)
- Display fonts for hero text
- Varied weights and styles
- Decorative touches (drop caps, etc.)

## Signature Elements
- Layered shadows (multiple)
- Gradient backgrounds/borders
- Glassmorphism where appropriate
- Hover animations (scale, glow)
- Custom cursors
- Parallax scrolling
